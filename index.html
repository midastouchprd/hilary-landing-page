My Story

Hilary Kuhlmey is a singer/songwriter who has been melting the hearts of those in the Austin music scene with her powerful voice. Her signature, soulful style has been compared to world-renowned artists Amy Winehouse, Duffy, and Adele. She has jump-started 2015 with her self-titled EP release and is planning for an upcoming national tour. Her emotional lyrics and nostalgic sound will bring tears to your eyes and send chills up your spine. After hitting a milestone and experiencing an awakening in her life, Hilary left her day job to pursue her life-long dream of music.

"If I can be the vessel that spreads the magic of music, then that is my greatest gift." ~HK
"Long after our bodies die ~our music~ lives on. Creation is timeless." ~HK
